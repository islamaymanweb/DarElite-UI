Our Journey

The Dar Elite Story

From a small workshop to a trusted name in luxury furniture

Founded in 2010 by Sarah Johnson, Dar Elite began as a passion project in a small garage workshop. Sarah's vision was simple yet profound: create furniture that doesn't just fill spaces, but transforms them into sanctuaries of comfort and style.

What started with custom pieces for friends and family quickly grew into a beloved local brand. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship, sustainable materials, and timeless design resonated with homeowners who valued both beauty and longevity.

Today, Dar Elite stands as a testament to what happens when passion meets purpose. Every piece in our collection carries the same attention to detail and love for design that started it all those years ago.
